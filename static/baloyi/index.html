Wine Tasting Services at Baloyi Black Wine Emporium

Indulge in the Sophistication of Our Wine Tasting Experience

At Baloyi Black Wine Emporium, we're not just about serving drinks; we're about creating an experience.

Here's what you can expect when you choose our wine tasting services:

  • Champagne
  • Sparkling wine / MCC / Prosecco
  • Red wine / Western Cape
  • White wine / Western Cape
  • Rose / South Africa / Spain

Complete Wine Tasting Setup

We provide all the essentials:

  • Wine Glasses & Flutes:
    For the perfect wine tasting experience.
  • Ice Buckets & Ice:
    To keep your selections at the ideal temperature.
  • Wine Openers & Stoppers:
    Ensuring every bottle is presented and preserved perfectly.
  • Complimentary Refreshments:
    Still and sparkling water to cleanse the palate
  • Elegant Decor:
    To set the perfect ambiance for your wine tasting event.

Premier Wine Bar Service

Our wine bar service is the centerpiece of your event. We bring a selection of the finest wines, each chosen for its unique character and flavor profile, ensuring a diverse and enriching tasting experience.

Expert Guidance by Two Sommeliers

Our experienced sommeliers are the heart of our wine tasting service. They're not just wine experts; they're storytellers who will guide you through the journey of each wine, sharing insights into the origins, tasting notes, and perfect pairings.

Attentive Service Tailored to Your Event

Depending on the scale and style of your tasting event, we provide either one or two professional waiters. Their focus is to ensure every guest feels attended to and the wine keeps flowing seamlessly.

Free Transportation in and around Johannesburg

As a bonus, we offer free transportation of our wine tasting setup to any location in and around Johannesburg. This ensures a hassle-free experience for you, letting you focus on enjoying the event.
